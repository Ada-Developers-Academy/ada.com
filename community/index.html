Host an Intern

There are many benefits to sponsoring Ada Developers Academy. As a sponsoring company, you’ll have access to Ada students for internships and potential hires. Please view our information for companies if you’re interested.

Software Developers

We’re always looking for mentors, guest speakers, and curriculum collaborators. If you’d like to participate in the Ada Developers Academy community, please send us an email.

In-Kind Donation

We’re running a busy classroom full of students and volunteers. If you or your company would like to make an in-kind donation (A/V equipment, supplies, snacks, coffee), please contact us!
