Get Involved

Individuals

The success of Ada Developers’ Academy is dependent on donation. We're starting a crowdsource funding campaign - check back soon!

Companies

There are many benefits to sponsoring Ada Developers' Academy. Please download our sponsorship prospectus. As a sponsoring company, you’ll have access to ADA students for apprenticeships and potential hires. Please contact us if you’re interested.

Software Developers

We’re always looking for mentors, guest speakers, and curriculum collaborators. If you’d like to participate in the Ada Developer Academy community, please send us an email.

In-Kind Donation

We’re running a busy classroom full of students and volunteers. If you or your company would like to make an in-kind donation (A/V equipment, supplies, snacks, coffee), please contact us!
