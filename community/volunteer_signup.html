Volunteer Information

Volunteering is super awesome. As a nonprofit, we depend on the generosity of our volunteers to help keep our program running smoothly.

Benefits of volunteering include:
  • Having a super fun time!
  • Gaining a deeper understanding of material you use professionally
  • Pride in actively contributing to the closing of the software gender gap
  • Meeting and networking with other technology professionals who share your passion for diversity

Volunteer Roles

In-class Teaching Assistant

This role is for web professionals who are confidently capable in the material that they are assisting with. They will spend 2-3 hours per week in the classroom, aiding students with their classwork. They can choose which weeks they'll help out with (based on their knowledge and experience), will sand will be prepped in advance with the curriculum and projects that will be worked on during their time in the classroom.

Remote Teaching Assistant

This role is for web or software professionals with a great deal of general development knowledge to share. They will hang out in the IRC chatroom for 2-3 hours a week, on their own schedule, aiding students with questions they may have about their classwork or general technical issues.

Community Contact

This role is for technical professionals. They will attend a monthly social event at the Ada classroom, share industry and life experience with students, and help students build their professional networks.
